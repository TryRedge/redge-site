FAQ

Which adapters?
Any quality BLE OBD‑II that speaks ELM327/PIDs cleanly. We'll publish a tested list before launch.
Platforms?
iOS first (Swift/SwiftUI). Android next.
Battery impact?
We pause sampling when the vehicle stops and keep reconnects efficient.
Which vehicles?
Works with both cars and motorcycles that support OBD‑II (most vehicles 1996+).
Data privacy?
Your data stays on your device. We don't track locations or sell data to third parties.
Pricing?
We're exploring freemium and premium tiers. Early adopters get special pricing.

Privacy Policy

Effective date:

We built Redge to be privacy‑first. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how you can control it.

1. Data we process

  • On‑device telemetry (e.g., RPM, speed, temperatures, derived scores). Stored locally by default.
  • App diagnostics (crash logs, basic analytics) to improve stability. You can opt out where supported.
  • Contact (email) when you join the waitlist or contact us.

2. How we use data

  • Provide real‑time and post‑session insights.
  • Maintain app reliability and security.
  • Communicate updates you opt into.

3. Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share minimal data with processors that help us run the app (e.g., crash reporting), under strict agreements.

4. Your choices

  • Export and delete your sessions at any time from the app.
  • Opt out of diagnostics where supported by your OS.
  • Contact us at privacy@tryredge.com for any request.

5. Retention

We retain contact emails until you unsubscribe and diagnostics per platform defaults (typically 90 days).

6. Children

Redge is not directed to children under 16.

7. Changes

We will update this policy as the product evolves. We'll change the effective date above and, when material, notify you.
